MATCHA CAKE

INGREDIENTS

Green tea preparation appropriate for cake.
Your own white sponge cake recipe, as this cake should be airy.
Cake topping of your choice, we recomend a dusting of Green Tea appropriate for baking.

DIRECTIONS

Add the Matcha to your dry ingredients and mix with your usual wet ingredients.
Bake as usual.

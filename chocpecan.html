CHOCOLATE PECAN PIE

INGREDIENTS

  • Pie crust, preferably one without sugar so as to ensure that the flavor of the pie is enhanced rather then overwelmed by a sugary taste. Our suggestion would be for one which uses cream cheese in combination with flour and butter.
  • Dark choclate, melted, with a small amount of butter.
  • Your usual combination of ingredients for the filling.
  • Pecans

DIRECTIONS

  • After baking the crust until just slightly brown, top it with the already melted chocolate and butter mix.
  • Add in some pecans, preferably pieces less then half size.
  • Add in your filling.
  • Top with pecan halves.
  • Bake as usual.
